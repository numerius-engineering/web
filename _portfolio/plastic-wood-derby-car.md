    <h2>Project focus</h2>
    <p>The goal was to build a derby car to regulation-style expectations while replacing the traditional wood body with a 3D-printed alternative. The result highlights how additive manufacturing can be used to reinterpret a familiar physical product while still respecting fit, weight, and performance constraints.</p>
    <h2>What it demonstrates</h2>
    <p>This build reflects a practical prototyping mindset: rapid fabrication, hands-on tuning, and iterative refinement in a real physical system. It also shows how fabrication choices influence downstream performance, especially when small adjustments and final assembly details can materially affect the outcome.</p>
